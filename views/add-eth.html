Tips for Successful ETH Transfers

Network Verification

Always select Ethereum Network (ERC-20). Other networks will result in lost funds.

Gas Fees

Check current Ethereum gas fees. High network congestion increases fees significantly.

Timing

Transfer during low traffic hours (UTC 2-6 AM) for lower fees and faster confirmations.

Address Verification

Verify first 6 and last 4 characters of address match before sending.

Test Transaction

For large amounts, send a small test transaction first to verify everything works.

Transaction History

Always save your transaction hash (TXID) for reference and support.
